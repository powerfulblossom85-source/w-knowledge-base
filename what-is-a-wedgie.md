# What is a Wedgie?

A **wedgie** is the act of pulling someone's underwear (typically from the back) upward with enough force to create a wedging effect between the buttocks. It can range from playful to more intense variations and is found in pranks, schoolyard humor, and consensual adult communities.

## Characteristics
- Involves underwear being yanked upward (briefs, boxers, thongs, etc.)
- Can be self-administered, or given by another person

## Common Wedgie Techniques & Variations

Here is a complete list of the most recognized techniques (compiled from community sources):

- **Classic / Regular Wedgie**  
  The basic back-pull: underwear is grabbed from behind and pulled upward between the buttocks.

- **Melvin (Frontal Wedgie)**  
  The front version of a classic wedgie — underwear is pulled up from the front, often causing more genital pressure.

- **Atomic Wedgie**  
  The underwear is pulled so high that the waistband goes over the victim’s head (or rips off). Considered the “granddaddy” of wedgies; works best with stretchy underwear.

- **Hanging Wedgie**  
  The victim is lifted and their underwear is hooked onto something high (hook, door, tree branch, etc.) so their feet dangle or barely touch the ground.

- **Dangling Wedgie**  
  Similar to hanging but the victim’s toes just barely reach the floor.

- **Bouncing Wedgie (Kangaroo Wedgie)**  
  The victim is given a wedgie and then bounced/jumped up and down while held by the underwear.

- **All-Around Wedgie**  
  Underwear is pulled up from the front, back, and both sides simultaneously (usually requires multiple people).

- **Jock-Lock Wedgie**  
  The victim’s feet are pulled back and tucked through the leg holes of their own underwear, locking them in place.

- **Backward Wedgie**  
  The underwear is worn backwards first, then wedgied — creates a thong-like effect in the back.

- **Messy Wedgie**  
  A wedgie combined with food, drink, or other substances poured down the pants/underwear.

- **Squeaky Clean Wedgie**  
  Frontal wedgie followed immediately by a back wedgie (or vice-versa) to create extra friction.

- **Bra-Connection Wedgie** (or Shoulder Wedgie)  
  The underwear is pulled up and hooked onto the back of a bra strap or over the shoulders.

- **Chair Wedgie**  
  The victim is seated and their underwear is hooked around the back of the chair.

- **Ripping Wedgie**  
  The underwear is pulled hard enough that it tears or rips off completely.

Other less common but documented variations include Propeller, Blast, Scarecrow, and Self-Wedgie versions of almost any type.
