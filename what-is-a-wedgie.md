# What is a Wedgie?

A **wedgie** is the act of pulling someone's underwear (typically from the back) upward with enough force to create a wedging effect between the buttocks. It can range from playful to more intense variations.

## Common Characteristics
- Involves underwear being yanked upward
- Often targets briefs, boxers, or thongs
- Can be self-administered, given by another person, or part of group activity

## Variations Preview
- Classic / Melvin (front-pull)
- Atomic (over-the-head)
- Hanging (suspended)
